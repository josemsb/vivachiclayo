Rated as the Best Places to Eat in Chiclayo
In order to determine the best restaurants in Chiclayo, we surveyed a number of people, and found that these were consistent favorites:
TRADITIONAL PERUVIAN
Sorrento
Famous for its empanadas, Sorrento is a favorite for many locals in Chiclayo. Be sure to arrive early to this restaurant because there can often be a wait.

Address: Bolognesi 334, Chiclayo              

Phone Number: (51)(074)-27-5099
Sabores Peruanos
Known for its consistently delicious traditional Peruvian food, this lunch-only restaurant will not disappoint lovers of Peruvian coastal cuisine.

Address: Av. Los Incas 136 La Victoria, Chiclayo           

Phone Number: (51)(074)-23-1018
El Rincon del Pato
A little farther away from Chiclayo, this restaurant is known by locals as the place to get true Peruvian arroz con pato.

Address: Augusto B. Leguía 270, Lambayeque          

Phone Number: (51)(074)- 28-2751
Hebron
The most recommended restaurant of our survey, Hebron is a mid-to-high price range restaurant that will give you quality food in a clean, quiet environment. If you’d prefer, many members of the staff speak English.

Address: J Balta 605, Chiclayo            

Phone Number: (51)(074)-23-9486
Fiesta
iesta is notorious for being the fanciest, most gourmet restaurant in town. Typical dishes include ceviche a la brasa, cabrito de leche, and arroz con pato.

Address: Av. Salaverry 1820, Chiclayo           

Phone Number:(51)(074)-20-1970

SEAFOOD:

Cerrito Norteño

Many locals refer to Cerrito Norteño as the best ceviche in Chiclayo. Fresh from the nearby Pacific Ocean, this restaurant offers a variety of delicious seafood dishes for around 20 soles.

Address: Ca. Piura Cuadra 4 Jose Olaya (Manuel Arteaga), Chiclayo

Phone Number: (51)(074)-23-3741
Seafood Casa Del Mar

Address: Located between Grau and Pacasmayo 100, Urb Santa Victoria, Chiclayo
El Tiburón

Address: 922 Misericordia, Monsefu          

 Phone Number: (51)(74)-41-1729
CAFE's:
Cafe 900
This restaurant has a nice, relaxing vibe about it, and serves lots of different types of food– everything from sandwiches and smoothies to pasta and Peruvian food. It often has local artists playing live music.

Address: Manuel Maria Izaga 900, Chiclayo          

 Phone Number: (51)(074)-20-9268
Cafe Astoria
This cafe is widely known as having the best sandwiches and tamales around. Because of this, they close when they are sold out, and therefore are usually only open from 8-10 AM and 7-10 PM each day.

Address: Bolognesi 627, Chiclayo          

Phone Number: (51)-978-785-816
D’Pulpa
A visit to D’Pulpa is something that you definitely do not want to miss out on. They sell hamburgers and sandwiches and have the widest selection of juices in town. Their menu contains pages and pages of juice combinations which will leave you wanting to come back again and again!

Address: Calle Los Pinos, 300 Santa Victoria, Chiclayo            

Phone Number: (51)(074)-20-8830

Website: dpulpa.com                  

Hours: Monday-Sunday, 8 AM- 1:30 PM and 5-11:30 PM
Kango Cafe
This posh cafe has a bright, energetic vibe and is a great place for a light meal or snacks. They sell juice, hot chocolate, salads, pizza, sandwiches, ice cream, fries, and other such foods. Close to the Centro, Kango Cafe is great for a quick snack and break from your day!

Address: Calle Elías Aguirre Nro 561, Chiclayo              

Phone Number: (51)-943-750-661
GRILLS:
Brasa Roja
You can always count on Brasa Roja to have delicious Pollo a la Brasa–their huge portions and tasty fries make them an easy choice for anyone who likes chicken.

Address: Avenida Santa Victoria 700, Chiclayo          

Phone Number: (51)(074)-23-5588
Marakos Grill
Succulent grilled meats.

Address: Avenida Elvira Garcia Y Garcia 490, Chiclayo             Phone Number: (51)(074)-23-2840

Website: marakosgrill.com
La Churrasqueria
Delicious ribs and fries, along with other barbequed meats.

Address: Los Alumnos 157 – Urb. San Luis, Chiclayo            

 Phone Number: (51)(074)-22-1746
Brother’s Burgers and BBQ
Owned and operated by a man from Colorado and his wife, Brother’s offers fantastic American-style burgers and ribs with a Peruvian twist at a very reasonable price. Check out their facebook page here.

Address: Av. Arizola N. 200 Urb. Federico Villarreal, Chiclayo                         Phone Number:(51)-950-836-445

Hours: 4-10 pm Monday-Saturday
