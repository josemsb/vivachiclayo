Combi: 

For the more adventurous type, one can always get on one of the minibuses or vans heading different directions. Walking along the street, vans will be driving around with someone yelling from the door or front seat the direction they are going. If you want to go to the same city, flag them down and ask the price. This is a very inexpensive way to travel where you want to go, and close cities are usually only a couple Soles.
