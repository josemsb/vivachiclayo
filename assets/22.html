Recommended Clubs and Karaoke Bars in Chiclayo

Like most Latin American countries, music and dance is a huge part of Peruvian culture. You haven’t truly experienced Peru until you have been to a Peruvian club or karaoke bar. Here are a few places we recommend, each with a different vibe:

Sabor y Son Cubano
For an older or more relaxed crowd, check out this Cuban jazz and salsa club.

Address: San Jose 155, Chiclayo

Hours:  Opens at 9 PM and goes late into the night on Fridays and Saturdays.

Phone Number: (51) (074)-27-2555
Magno
This is a club that will keep you dancing all night long! It has an open bar for the ladies until midnight, and is a great place to dance with the locals of Chiclayo.

Address: 490 Av.José Leonardo Ortiz, Chiclayo

Hours: Fridays and Saturdays from 10:30 on

Phone Number: (51) (074)- 23-6266

Website: www.magno.pe
Rustica
Providing shows, a restaurant, bar, disco, and karaoke, Rustica is a chill club with delicious food and a spot for everyone to enjoy themselves

Address: Av Miguel de Cervantes n. 300, Chiclayo (in Real Plaza)

Hours: Sunday-Thursday, 12 PM – 3 AM; Friday and Saturday, 12 PM – 5 AM (Karaoke 5:30 PM – 3 AM)

Phone Number:(51)(074)- 23-6992 or 221367

Website: rustica.com.pe
