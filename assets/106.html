Typical Prices: 

Though the price of transportation can be influenced by the time of year, specials, and other factors, these are our best estimates of costs:

 

-Flight from Lima to Chiclayo: $175 (~470 Soles) each way

-Flight from Chiclayo to Cuzco: $600-$800 (~1600- 2100 Soles) round trip or $300-$500 (~800-1300 Soles) One-way

-Bus from Lima- Chiclayo: $40 (~90 Soles) each way

-Bus from Chiclayo-Trujillo: $10 (~27 Soles) each way

-Bus from Chiclayo-Cajamarca: $20 (~56 Soles) each way

-Bus from Chiclayo-Piura: $12 (~32 Soles) each way

-Mototaxi within Chiclayo: 2-3  Soles (~$.80- $1.10)

-Taxi within Chiclayo: 3-6 Soles (~$1.10- $2.20)

-Taxi an hour outside Chiclayo (and back): $17 (~45 Soles) (unless they wait for you at the site, then it will be more)

-Combi anywhere within 30 minutes: ~2 soles ($.80)
