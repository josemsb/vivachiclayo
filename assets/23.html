Books Stacked to the Ceiling:
This street is famous for its book-packed shops. As you enter a bookshop on this street you will see stacks of books towering over you. The books cover the shelves on the wallsall the way to the ceiling! It seems like the majority of the books were collected out of schools, attics or old libraries, so it makes for a great place to find vintage books in Spanish. It is also an easy place to find textbooks and books written by famous South American novelists and poets. Visit the Book Street! It is a fun experience and you never know what treasures you might find.
Location
Close to the Mercado Modelo, the cross-streets are Alfonso Ugarte and Arica or Manuel Pardo and Arica.
