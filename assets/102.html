Bus Stations:

Taking the bus is a great alternative to flying, if you have the time. In addition to the significant amount of money you will save, it allows you to see the beautiful countryside along the way. The average cost of a one-way ticket from Lima to Chiclayo (a 12-hour ride) is about 90 Soles (~$40). There are also buses making regular trips to and from Chiclayo from almost any large city in Peru. You will need to purchase tickets beforehand and have your passport handy when boarding. Cruz del Sur, Oltursa, and Linea usually have the most comfortable, dependable buses. Most of them have large, cushioned seats that recline back almost all the way, with a footrest, small pillow, and blanket provided. Check them out and reserve a seat here:
