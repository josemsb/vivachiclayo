Artisan Work from Start to Finish:

This artisan market-the official name being “Parque Artesanal Atin”- is in the sleepy town of Eten and could easily be passed up if you did not know what you were looking for. As you walk inside, you find a courtyard surrounded by various shops. Each artisan has their own little shop, where they sell the handmade items they have personally created.

Right when you walk in, there is a large room to the left where they do most of their weaving. Here, the women are excited to show you their work, and may even let you have a try at weaving if you would like! Visiting this market is fun, and is a breath of fresh air compared to other tourist-oriented shops. You have the chance to see the whole weaving process, buy some souvenirs, and converse with the actual people whose life work is to make these items!

Location
At Av. Mscal. Castilla in the city of Eten, which is about 12 miles (20 km) from Chiclayo. This is about a 25-minute drive.
