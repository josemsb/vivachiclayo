Artwork from the Locals:

 This building– the “Casa de Cultura”– is surrounded by a garden and statues, and is even more beautiful  inside. It houses the work of local artists– everything from paintings and sketches to stone sculptures and music. The exhibits displayed usually have some sort of theme that each piece pertains to.

The Casa de Cultura has one room for artwork, and often has different types of concerts and workshops held in the back of the building. Stop by, marvel at the artwork produced by the locals, and see if any events are scheduled during your time there!

Location
Av. Luis Gonzales 345, Chiclayo
